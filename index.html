Some of My Work

UW COVID Data Science Hackathon

Winner of the inaugural hackathon hosted by Washington's MSDS program. Worked with a remote team to generate visualizations that compare and analyze how COVID-19 has spread throughout the world using Python and Tableau

Using GAN in QT Ultrasound Imaging

Employed deep learning and computer vision to implement the unsupervised learning algorithm, Generative Adversarial Networks (GAN), which we used to generate realistic ultrasound images. The data agumentation allowed imputation of missing data and sharper imaging, facilitating more accurate detection of breast tumors, using a Pytorch framework and NVIDIA GPUs. We currently have a patent pending on the application of this technology.

Recommender Systems

Assisted several startup companies in developing recommender systems for their respective products. Worked on utilizing social media APIs to scrape profiles for users' interests. Worked with team to flesh out use-cases and streamline design flow.

Time Series Algorithm for Predicting Long Term Energy Prices

Built a dynamic time series model to predict energy price trends by evaluating risks in renewable energy infrastructure, with an emphasis on long-term accuracy and adaptability. Used BeautifulSoup to scrape 15 years worth of unstructured data, and build an ARIMA model

Network Science

Current project: working on utilizing the field of network science to examine how coronavirus is spreading
