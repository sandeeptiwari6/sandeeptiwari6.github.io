About Me

I am a recent graduate from UC Berkeley, and I'm currently pursuing my Master's in Data Science from the University of Washington. During my free time, I like to work on personal projects and improve my own skills, as steps to becoming the best version of myself that I can be.

As a data scientist, I hope to be able to utilize my skillset to automate tools and processes in the medical sector. I am also very interested in applying ML and AI in the financial sphere, to create extremely accurate predictive models and analyze economic trends.

My goal is to provide value to society. Data is my pathway towards that goal

MY HOBBIES

Sports

I'm a big sports guy. Tennis was the first sport I took interest in as a kid, sparking my competetive nature. I continued playing competetively through high school, but have been playing more recreationally since then. I eventually began to follow professional tennis very closely, constantly tracking the stats of my favorite player, Roger Federer.

While tennis will always be my #1, I also like the collaborative nature of other team sports, like basketball and football. Whenever I can, I try to rally a group of friends and run pickup games at the local park or gym. And of course, I play in my fantasy football league every year, watching NFL games every Sunday.

Music

Music has been one of the most significant influences in my life. Listening ato and playing different tunes helps me de-stress, relax and can also help motivate me in trying times. While listening to music is a great escape, I've found that singing is something that truly makes me happy, and so I try to sing whenever I can.

I was in my high school jazz choir, and at Cal, I was a part of Dil Se, UC Berkeley's premier South Asian a cappella team. Nowadays, I continue singing by jamming with friends, or singing in the shower.

Travel

I've always enjoyed travelling and have had a desire to see the world. Immersifying myself in other cultures, particularly those different from what I'm accustomed to, is an opportunity I will always relish. As someone who loves to be active, I truly hope I can live a life where I can see different parts of the world

Although I've visited my fair share of destinations, there are so many things I still have on my travel bucket list: coral reef scuba diving in the Great Barrier Reef, skydiving in New Zealand, eating sushi in Japan, Oktoberfest in Germany—you name it
